About Us

Pooches & Pastries was created out of our love for dogs. We believe dogs should have a place to go out to eat too! We strive to serve only healthy treats to your beloved pooches. We use natural ingredients and gluten-free products, creating delicious snacks and entrees. Our menu changes periodically as to keep things fresh and exciting. And we haven't forgotten their humans serving freshly baked pastries. Please let us know what we can do to make your doggie's experience special and memorable.
